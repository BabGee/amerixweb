TRANSLOADING SERVICES

With our transloading services, Amerix will receive and process your rail or ocean freight container and load your products onto trailers for final outbound distribution.

  • Dedicated Contract Carriage
  • LTL RETAIL DISTRIBUTION
  • TEMPERATURE-CONTROLLED
  • POOL DISTRIBUTION
  • FREIGHT CONSOLIDATION
  • TRANSPORTATION LOGISTICS

AMERIX ’S TRANSLOADING SERVICES ARE:

Fast: We’ll strip containers quickly and get them back to the terminal to avoid penalties.

Integrated: Amerix can pick up your container, unload it at our local warehouse, put it onto a Amerix truck, and handle final delivery.

Close to ports: The proximity of Amerix warehouses to Kenyan ports means you can get products to market faster.

For a major manufacturer of confectionery products, Amerix receives rail containers of temp-controlled products from around the country, unloads them at a Amerix warehouse in Mombasa and Nairobi stores them awaiting outbound orders, and then loads trucks for final delivery.

ADVANTAGES OF AMERIX ’S TRANSLOADING SERVICES:

  • Speed distribution cycle time and improve your cash flow.
  • Reduce dray costs since many Amerix DCs are located minutes from major West Coast ports.
  • Eliminate errors and delays – when you use Amerix to handle all 3PL services between the port and the final mile, you remove the communication and coordination errors that result from working with multiple providers.
