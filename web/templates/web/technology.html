At Amerix, our approach to technology is about speed and flexibility – from both our 3PL systems and our systems staff – enabling rapid implementation and customization. Our 3PL systems include:

Warehouse Management. Amerix uses Synapse as its Warehouse Management System (WMS) – a completely RF-driven system for accurate, paperless processing.

Transportation Management. Amerix uses MercuryGate as its Transportation Management System (TMS), which delivers rapid onboarding, customized reports and a customer portal to track shipments, pull PODs and get rates.

Electronic Data Interchange (EDI). Amerix has in-house EDI development professionals to help you navigate your specific needs and provide custom solutions.

Ecommerce Integrations. Amerix systems integrate with a variety of ecommerce platforms including the following:


Customer Portal for Supply Chain Visibility. Amerix’s online portal allows you to check inventory levels, download a BOL or POD, and check real-time order and receipt status using your own reference or purchase order numbers. You can also download a variety of information and analytical views directly from the portal.

Robust Reporting. Amerix uses SAP’s Business Intelligence Launch Pad to organize and present a core library of reports that deliver real-time data related to orders and inventory – in PDF and Excel formats. We offer custom development for new and existing reports. All reports are available ad hoc and can be scheduled for delivery, and KPIs can be fully customized.
