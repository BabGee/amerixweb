AMERIX ’S CONFECTIONERY WAREHOUSE SERVICES ARE:

Proven: Decades of experience serving food and confectionery companies.

Integrated: Single-source solution for cold chain distribution, including temp-controlled drayage, warehousing and final delivery.

Compliant: Amerix ’s food-grade facilities have received a superior rating from AIB International, while systems and processes exceed FDA and TTB requirements, including FSMA and HACCP.

For a large, overseas maker of candy products, Amerix manages all components of distribution, from transportation from the port to final retail delivery. Integrated systems allow the company’s logistics staff to easily track product from the time it hits the port.

ADVANTAGES OF AMERIX’S CONFECTIONERY WAREHOUSE AND LOGISTICS:

  • Maintain product integrity by carefully monitoring warehouse and trailer temperatures against requirements.
  • Access refrigerated trucking capacity – Amerix is the only time-defined, asset-based, temperature-controlled LTL carrier in Kenya
  • Reduce costs through freight consolidation services by letting us combine your deliveries with those of other Amerix customers that share the same retail destinations and temperature-range requirements.
  • Carefully monitor inventory by expiration date and use our system to ship according to FEFO, FIFO and other protocols.
