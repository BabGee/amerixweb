The Chief Executive Officer

Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Duncan Ombura

Mr. Duncan Ombura holds Double Major Bachelor of Commerce degree (Purchasing & Business Logistics) and (Management Science) from the Daystar University.

He holds Diploma in Business Administration & Management from the same University, in addition to Certificate in Fire Engineering from the Technical University of Kenya and Diploma in French from the Alliance Français de Nairobi.

He is an associate member of the Kenya Institute of Supplies Management (KISM)

Mr. Ombura is a trained ISO Lead Auditor and an Instructor of Quality Wise Programs. He aspires to enroll into an MBA-Strategic Management degree in due course.

Mr. Ombura previously worked in a busy Transport and Logistics department of a leading healthcare provider in East and Central Africa in the country for over 8 years. He is also a trained and an active Paramedic volunteer with the St. John Ambulance Brigade for the last 20 years.

He speaks French and plays Hockey.
