HOW PRODUCTS GET TO MARKET FASTER IN KENYA

Your products aren’t worth anything until they’re on a retail shelf or a consumer’s doorstep. At Amerix Logistics, it’s our job to get these products to market faster. ABOUT US

PORT SERVICES

With Amerix’s port services, you can rely on one provider to accelerate your regional logistics – from port to market. Containers are picked up by Amerix drivers and Amerix trucks. Our knowledge of terminal pick-up procedures at the port of Mombasa and in Nairobi means we can move goods quickly from port to final distribution.

WAREHOUSE SERVICES

Amerix operates about 2 million square feet of space across 11 kenyan distribution centers. Warehouses are strategically located near some of the world’s busiest ports and offer storage solutions for a wide range of industries including chemical, retail, food and beverage, and consumer goods.
