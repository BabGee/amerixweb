TRANSPORTATION

Final-mile delivery to mass retailers and grocery chains in Nairobi and Mombasa

In addition to providing warehousing and port services, Ameerix Logistics is a Kenyan trucking company that specializes in final-mile delivery to mass retailers and grocery chains in Nairobi and Mombasa. Our fixed-asset fleet delivers dry and temp-controlled loads direct throughout the region, with next-day service to your Nairobi and Mombasa customers. Through our non-asset transportation logistics group, we handle shipments coast to coast.
