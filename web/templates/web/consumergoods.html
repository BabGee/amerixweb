CPG LOGISTICS

Consumer product companies trust Amerix for accurate, on-time distribution to retail customers. Our CPG logistics services include rapid replenishment of retail and consumer orders. Amerix’s specialty: vendor compliance management to avoid chargebacks.

AMERIX’S CPG LOGISTICS SERVICES ARE:

Flexible: Space and labor can fluctuate with demand, so your distribution costs parallel your revenue stream.

Integrated: Amerix ’s drayage, warehousing and transportation can be combined into a single-source distribution solution in Kenya.

Proven: Amerix has drop-trailer agreements and standing delivery appointments with every major retail in the region. It’s likely that we already have strong relationships and pre-built system integrations with your most important retail customers.

Advanced: Access expert value- added services– ticketing, labeling, kitting, repacking, final product configuration – to make your products “retail-ready.”

ADVANTAGES OF AMERIX’S CPG LOGISTICS SERVICES:

  • Avoid chargebacks using Amerix dedicated quality and compliance team, which monitors routing guide changes and ensures outbound shipments comply with individual retailer requirements.
  • Gain flexibility using Amerix’s 12 warehouses to expand and contract your operations with business volumes.
  • Get the attention you need – don’t get lost at a large-scale 3PL that’s too busy to focus on you.
  • Cut freight costs with pool distribution – linehaul products to Mombasa, where Amerix breaks down shipments and manages last-mile delivery to your Region customers.
  • Manage B2B and B2C fulfillment from the same distribution center – minimize inventory by replenishing all your channels, including eCommerce fulfilment from the same inventory pool.

CPG LOGISTICS EXPERTISE

  • Direct store deliveries or DC deliveries to Walmart, Target, and other major retailers
  • Promotion support, including fast ramp up for high-volume requirements
  • Deconsolidation of imports near Southern Mombasa port.
  • Inventory management with a variety of disciplines – date code, FIFO, LIFO, batch codes
  • Recall management
  • Full supply chain visibility for your import freight – from notification of container availability to final retail delivery
